What we offer

Everything your brand needs. One studio.

We cover print, brand identity, and digital design so you never need three different vendors to tell one coherent story.

01. Print

Custom printing & branded production

Fast, quality printing for businesses across Ghana. We proof before we print, and we deliver to your door.

  • Business essentials including cards, flyers, brochures, invitations, and posters
  • Merchandise such as branded t-shirts, mugs, stickers, and labels
  • Large format printing covering roll-up banners, signage, and event backdrops
  • Delivery to Tema, Kumasi, Takoradi, and across Ghana

02. Brand

Brand identity & business positioning

A logo is not a brand. We build the full identity covering how your business looks, speaks, and shows up for customers.

  • Identity work covering logo direction, colour systems, and typography
  • Messaging development so you know what to say and how to say it
  • Positioning strategy that gives customers a clear reason to choose you
  • Brand guidelines that keep your whole team consistent
Start a brand project
03. Digital

UI/UX, landing pages & digital tools

We design screens that convert and tools that work, whether you need a simple lead form or a full web product.

  • UI/UX design for apps, web products, and dashboards
  • Landing pages built for campaigns and launches that convert visitors into customers
  • Forms and surveys set up on Google Forms, Typeform, Tally, and custom flows
  • Social assets including graphics, templates, and campaign visuals
Discuss a digital project
04. Social Media

Social media management

Your brand exists beyond print. We manage your social presence so it stays active, consistent, and on-brand without you having to think about it every day.

  • Content creation covering graphics, captions, and post design in your brand style
  • Scheduling and posting at a consistent cadence across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn
  • Campaign support for launches, promotions, and event coverage
  • Profile setup including branded bios, covers, highlights, and link-in-bio pages
Get a social media quote
05. Surveys

Digital surveys for companies

We design, build, and set up digital surveys that actually get responses, whether the purpose is customer feedback, employee research, event registration, or product insight.

  • Customer feedback surveys that reveal what your clients actually think
  • Market research surveys that collect reliable data to support your business decisions
  • Event registration forms handling sign-ups, RSVPs, and attendance tracking
  • Platform setup on Google Forms, Typeform, Tally, and SurveyMonkey
Discuss a survey project
06. Landing Pages and Forms

Landing pages & marketing forms

We design and set up your full lead capture system, from the page people land on to the form that captures them to the notification that reaches you.

  • Launch landing pages for product launches, event sign-ups, and campaign pages
  • Lead capture forms designed to turn visitors into enquiries
  • Marketing form setup that is built, embedded, and connected directly to your workflow
  • QR code integration that connects your printed materials directly to your digital forms
