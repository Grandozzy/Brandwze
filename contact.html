FAQ

Common questions

Do you only work in Accra?

We deliver and fulfil orders across Accra, Tema, Kumasi, and Takoradi. Digital work is handled remotely anywhere in Ghana or beyond.

Can you design my file before printing it?

Yes. We support layout, brand direction, print preparation, and digital proofing before final production. Add design review when submitting your request.

Can I upload my own design?

Yes. The Print and Quote page is made exactly for this. Upload your file, select your specs, and submit. We will confirm and begin production.

Do you design websites, apps, and forms?

Yes. UI/UX design, landing pages, surveys, forms, and digital brand touchpoints are all part of our services alongside print production.

How fast is turnaround?

Standard is 3–5 business days. Rush jobs (24–48hr) are available for an additional fee. Tell us your deadline and we'll work around it.
